<p><strong>The Glow Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroresonant</li><li>Tap Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glow tube market types include ferroresonant and tap-switching systems. Ferroresonant glow tubes utilize a transformer design that stabilizes voltage and provides power factor correction, making them suitable for applications requiring consistent performance under varying loads. On the other hand, tap-switching glow tubes use multiple transformer tap settings to regulate voltage, allowing for energy savings and adaptability in diverse operating conditions. Both types enhance reliability and efficiency in lighting and signaling applications while addressing specific operational needs.</p></p>
