<p><p>The Host Cell Protein Testing market primarily includes PCR-based assays and ELISA-based assays. PCR-based assays are molecular techniques that detect specific DNA sequences from host cells, providing high sensitivity and specificity in identifying contaminants. In contrast, ELISA-based assays utilize antibodies to quantify host cell proteins, offering a reliable measure of contamination levels. Both methods are essential for ensuring the safety and efficacy of biopharmaceutical products by monitoring unwanted proteins that may affect drug performance.</p></p>
