<p><p>The Micro X-ray Fluorescence Analyzer market comprises two main types: contact and non-contact analyzers. Contact type X-ray fluorescence analyzers require direct application on the sample surface for accurate chemical composition analysis, offering high sensitivity and resolution. In contrast, non-contact X-ray fluorescence analyzers employ a remote approach, allowing for the analysis of samples without physical contact, making them ideal for delicate or valuable specimens. Both types serve various industries, including materials science, archaeology, and environmental monitoring, enhancing analytical capabilities.</p></p>
