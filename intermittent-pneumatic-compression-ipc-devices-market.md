<p><p>Intermittent Pneumatic Compression (IPC) devices are used primarily for the prevention and treatment of Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) and Pulmonary Embolism (PE). DVT refers to blood clots forming in deep veins, often in the legs, while PE occurs when these clots travel to the lungs, causing serious complications. IPC devices enhance venous blood flow by rhythmically inflating and deflating sleeves wrapped around the legs, promoting circulation and reducing the risk of these conditions, thereby being crucial in both hospital and outpatient settings.</p></p>
