<p><p>The hydrocolloid tape market is primarily divided into two types: woven single-coated hydrocolloid tape and non-woven single-coated hydrocolloid tape. In contrast, non-woven single-coated hydrocolloid tape is made from soft, flexible materials, providing excellent conformability and cushioning, which is ideal for sensitive skin or wound care. Both types are effective for moisture retention and promote a healing environment.</p></p>
