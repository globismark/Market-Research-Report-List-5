<p><p>Hygiene films are essential products used in various sectors like healthcare, food packaging, and personal care. There are two main types in the market: Micro-Porous Hygiene Films and Non-Porous Hygiene Films. Micro-Porous films allow air and moisture to pass through while blocking out bacteria and other contaminants, making them ideal for medical applications. On the other hand, Non-Porous films create a barrier against liquids and microorganisms, suitable for food packaging and personal hygiene products. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining cleanliness and preventing infections.</p></p>
