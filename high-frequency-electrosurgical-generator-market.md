<p><strong>The High Frequency Electrosurgical Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Circuit</li><li>Bipolar Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Frequency Electrosurgical Generator market includes two primary types: Monopolar and Bipolar circuits. Monopolar circuits use a single electrode to deliver high-frequency energy, allowing targeted tissue cutting and coagulation, making them ideal for larger surgical areas. In contrast, Bipolar circuits utilize two electrodes to pass energy between them, providing more precise control over smaller tissue areas with reduced thermal spread, making them suitable for delicate procedures. Both are essential in enhancing surgical efficiency and outcomes.</p></p>
