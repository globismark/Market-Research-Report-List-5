<p><strong>The Flange Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integral Flange</li><li>Threaded Flange</li><li>Butt Welding Flange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flange market consists of various types, including integral, threaded, and butt welding flanges. Integral flanges are forged as a single unit with the pipe, offering enhanced strength and reliability. Threaded flanges have internal threads that allow easy assembly onto pipes without welding, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. Butt welding flanges, designed to be welded to the pipe ends, provide a strong, permanent connection ideal for high-pressure environments. Each type serves distinct applications within industrial piping systems.</p></p>
