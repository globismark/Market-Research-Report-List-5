<p><p>The market for seatbelt sensors includes three main types: two-point safety belts, three-point safety belts, and four-point safety belts. Two-point safety belts are simple lap belts, primarily used in light-duty applications. Three-point safety belts incorporate a shoulder strap, offering enhanced occupant protection and are widely used in passenger vehicles. Four-point safety belts feature shoulder and lap straps that provide superior security, often used in performance vehicles and racing applications. Each type serves different safety requirements and market segments.</p></p>
