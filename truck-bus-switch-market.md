<p><p>The Truck and Bus Switch market is primarily categorized into touch type and press type switches. Touch type switches utilize capacitive or resistive technology, allowing users to activate functions with a simple touch. They offer sleek designs and enhanced aesthetics, making them popular in modern vehicles. On the other hand, press type switches require a physical push to operate, providing tactile feedback that is often preferred for its reliability and ease of use in various driving conditions. Both types cater to different user preferences and technological advancements.</p></p>
