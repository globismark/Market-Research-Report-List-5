<p><p>Resistance welding electrode holders are essential tools in welding applications, categorized into insulated and non-insulated types. Insulated holders prevent electrical current leakages, ensuring safety and improving weld quality by minimizing heat transfer to the operator. Non-insulated holders provide direct electrical contact for efficient welding, suitable for applications where high conductivity is critical. Each type serves specific operational needs, influencing factors like weld strength, energy efficiency, and user safety, making them vital in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
