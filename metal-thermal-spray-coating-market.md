<p><p>The Metal Thermal Spray Coating market includes two primary types: the Combustion Flame Process and the Electrical Process. The Combustion Flame Process utilizes a fuel gas and oxygen to create a flame that melts the coating material, which is then propelled onto the substrate. In contrast, the Electrical Process employs an electric arc or plasma to melt the coating material, allowing for more precise control and finer coatings. Both methods are essential for enhancing the durability and performance of various industrial components.</p></p>
