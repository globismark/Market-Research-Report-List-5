<p><p>The spray dryer market is segmented into three main types: pressure spray dryers, stream spray dryers, and centrifuging spray dryers. Pressure spray dryers utilize high pressure to atomize liquid feed, promoting rapid drying and preserving product quality. Stream spray dryers operate by injecting liquid into a heated gas stream, enhancing drying efficiency. Centrifuging spray dryers use centrifugal force to atomize the feed, allowing for uniform particle size and thorough drying. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and product characteristics.</p></p>
