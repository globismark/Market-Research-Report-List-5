<p><p>Veneer-faced panels are engineered wood products used in furniture manufacturing, with two main types: particleboard-faced and MDF-faced. Particleboard-faced panels are made from compressed wood chips and resin, offering a cost-effective option with decent durability and a smooth finish, often used in low to mid-range furniture. MDF-faced panels, composed of fine wood fibers, provide a denser, smoother surface and superior finish quality, making them ideal for high-end furniture applications, facilitating intricate designs and superior paint adhesion.</p></p>
