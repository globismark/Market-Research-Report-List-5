<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Type</li><li>Axial Flow Type</li><li>Inclinedflow Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high pressure draught fan market is segmented into three main types: centrifugal, axial flow, and inclined flow fans. Centrifugal fans use a rotating impeller to increase air pressure, making them suitable for systems requiring high resistance. Axial flow fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, ideal for scenarios needing substantial airflow with lower pressure. Inclined flow fans offer a compromise, directing air at an angle, useful in specific industrial applications where space or design constraints exist. Each type caters to distinct operational needs.</p></p>
