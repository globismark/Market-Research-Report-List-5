<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Head-up Display (HUD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-HUD</li><li>W-HUD</li><li>AR-HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Head-up Display (HUD) technology is categorized into three main types: C-HUD, W-HUD, and AR-HUD. C-HUD, or Combiner HUD, projects information onto a small transparent screen located in front of the driver. W-HUD, or Windshield HUD, displays information directly on the windshield itself, allowing the driver to see information without taking their eyes off the road. AR-HUD, or Augmented Reality HUD, superimposes information onto the driver's view of the real world, providing enhanced spatial awareness and navigation guidance.</p></p>
