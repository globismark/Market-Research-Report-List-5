Exponential Decay Pulse delivers short, high-intensity pulses with decreasing amplitude over time. Square Wave Pulse provides square-shaped pulses with uniform amplitude and duration. These different market types offer varying approaches to electropermeabilization in the field of microbiology.</p></p>
