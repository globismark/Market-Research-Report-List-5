<p><p>The optical fiber arc fusion splicer market consists of several types, primarily categorized as core alignment type, V-groove type, and others. Core alignment splicers use precise mechanics to align the fiber cores for optimal connectivity, ensuring low splice loss and high performance. V-groove types utilize grooves to stabilize the fibers during splicing, offering a more cost-effective solution while sacrificing some precision. Other types encompass innovations, such as passive alignment and advanced automated systems, catering to diverse applications and user needs.</p></p>
