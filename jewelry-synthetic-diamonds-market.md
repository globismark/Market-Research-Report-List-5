<p><ul><li>CVD</li><li>HPHT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jewelry synthetic diamond market consists mainly of two types: CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) and HPHT (High-Pressure High-Temperature). CVD diamonds are produced by chemically vaporizing carbon gases, creating high-quality gemstones with fewer impurities. Conversely, HPHT diamonds mimic natural diamond formation conditions by applying extreme pressure and temperature, resulting in stones that can exhibit unique characteristics. Both methods offer ethical and environmentally friendly alternatives to mined diamonds, appealing to consumers seeking sustainable options.</p></p>
