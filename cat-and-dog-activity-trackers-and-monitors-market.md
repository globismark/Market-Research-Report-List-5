<p><p>The Cat and Dog Activity Trackers and Monitors market can be categorized into GPS-based and radio-based systems. In contrast, radio-based trackers rely on short-range communication, often using Bluetooth or RF signals, to monitor pets' activities within a limited area, suitable for indoor tracking and activity monitoring. Both types aim to enhance pet safety and owner awareness.</p></p>
