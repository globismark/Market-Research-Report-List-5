<p><p>Flatwire hairpin motors are used in various market types including hairpin winding, double-ended welding winding, and wave winding. Hairpin winding involves using flatwire coils that are arranged in a hairpin shape, allowing for higher power density and efficiency. Double-ended welding winding involves welding flatwire coils at both ends, providing improved thermal performance and reliability. Wave winding utilizes flatwire coils that are wound in a wave pattern, offering better heat dissipation and performance under high stress conditions. Each market type offers unique advantages based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
