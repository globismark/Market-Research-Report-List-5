<p><p>The vascular access market encompasses various catheter types crucial for medical procedures. Central Venous Catheters (CVC) are used for long-term medication delivery and blood sampling. Implantable ports offer a discreet, long-term solution for patients requiring frequent treatment. Dialysis catheters facilitate hemodialysis for kidney patients, allowing efficient blood filtration. Peripherally Inserted Central Catheters (PICC) are ideal for extended intravenous therapy, providing reduced infection risks and easy access. Each type addresses specific patient needs and procedural requirements, enhancing overall care.</p></p>
