<p><p>The corrugated paper market is primarily divided into two types: single corrugated cardboard and double corrugated cardboard. Single corrugated cardboard consists of one layer of fluted paper sandwiched between two flat linerboards, offering lightweight packaging ideal for shipping and storage. In contrast, double corrugated cardboard features two layers of fluted paper with three linerboards, providing enhanced strength and durability, making it suitable for heavier or fragile items. Both types are widely used in various industries for packaging solutions.</p></p>
