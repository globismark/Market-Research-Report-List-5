<p><p>The electrostatic precipitator market is divided into two main types: dry and wet electrostatic precipitators. Dry electrostatic precipitators use high-voltage electrodes to charge and capture particles from flue gases in a dry environment, making them ideal for industries needing efficient dust removal without moisture. In contrast, wet electrostatic precipitators use liquid to enhance particle collection, effectively removing fine particulate matter and soluble pollutants, making them suitable for applications dealing with high moisture content or sticky materials.</p></p>
