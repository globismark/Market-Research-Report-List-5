<p><p>Water-based printing inks encompass various types, including flexo inks, gravure inks, and screen printing inks. Flexo inks are ideal for high-speed printing on flexible substrates, offering quick drying times and vibrant colors. Gravure inks, known for their excellent color fidelity and consistency, are commonly used for long-run printing, particularly in packaging. Screen printing inks, versatile and thick, provide excellent opacity and durability, making them suitable for textiles and specialty applications. Together, these inks serve diverse printing needs while promoting environmentally friendly practices.</p></p>
