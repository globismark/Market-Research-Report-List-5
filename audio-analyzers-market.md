<p><p>The audio analyzers market can be categorized into three main types: dual channel, four channel, and modular systems. Dual channel analyzers allow simultaneous measurement of two audio signals, making them suitable for basic testing tasks. Four channel analyzers expand on this by enabling the analysis of four signals concurrently, providing more comprehensive testing capabilities. Modular analyzers offer flexibility, allowing users to customize and expand their systems with various components to meet specific testing requirements, enhancing functionality and adaptability.</p></p>
