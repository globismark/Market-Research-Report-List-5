<p><strong>The Fiber Coupled Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emitter-Based FCM</li><li>Bar-Based FCM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Coupled Modules (FCM) come in two primary types: Emitter-Based FCM and Bar-Based FCM. Emitter-Based FCM utilizes individual laser diodes coupled to optical fibers, providing high precision and flexibility for various applications such as telecommunications and medical devices. Bar-Based FCM, on the other hand, integrates multiple laser diodes arranged in a bar format, allowing for higher power output and efficiency, making it suitable for industrial and defense applications. Both types cater to diverse market needs in fiber optics technology.</p></p>
