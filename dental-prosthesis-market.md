<p><p>The dental prosthesis market encompasses various types, including conventional full dentures, immediate full dentures, and partial dentures or overdentures. Conventional full dentures are custom-made to replace all missing teeth after healing. Immediate full dentures are inserted right after tooth removal, offering a solution during the healing phase. Partial dentures or overdentures are designed to fill gaps caused by missing teeth while preserving remaining natural teeth. These options cater to diverse patient needs, enhancing oral function and aesthetics.</p></p>
