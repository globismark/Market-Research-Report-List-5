<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automobile Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Column EPS，C-EPS</li><li>Pinion EPS，P-EPS</li><li>Rack EPS，R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile Electric Power Steering (EPS) utilizes different types of systems to assist in steering. Column EPS uses an electric motor attached to the steering column. C-EPS integrates the motor with the steering gear, improving efficiency. Pinion EPS places the motor directly on the steering pinion. P-EPS has the motor mounted on the rack, enhancing control. Rack EPS positions the motor on the steering rack for precise steering. Lastly, R-EPS combines the motor with the steering rod, providing responsive steering capabilities.</p></p>
