<p><p>The scooter tyre market features primarily three types: Bias, Radial, and Others. Bias tyres have layers arranged diagonally, offering flexibility and a softer ride, making them ideal for urban commuting. Radial tyres, with plies arranged perpendicular to the tread, provide better stability, fuel efficiency, and longevity, suitable for high-speed performance. The "Other" category includes specialty tyres designed for specific conditions, such as off-road or all-weather usage, catering to diverse customer needs and preferences within the scooter segment.</p></p>
