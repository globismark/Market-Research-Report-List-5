<p><strong>The Automatic Car Wash System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gantry Car Wash System</li><li>Conveyor Tunnel System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic car wash system market primarily features two types: Gantry Car Wash Systems and Conveyor Tunnel Systems. Gantry systems utilize a fixed structure that moves over the stationary vehicle, providing thorough cleaning with minimal space requirements. In contrast, Conveyor Tunnel Systems involve a moving conveyor belt that transports vehicles through a series of washing stages, offering a high-volume, efficient cleaning process. Both systems cater to various customer needs, emphasizing speed, effectiveness, and reduced labor costs.</p></p>
