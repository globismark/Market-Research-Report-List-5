<p><p>The refrigerated ice pack market comprises two primary types: disposable and reusable ice packs. Reusable ice packs, on the other hand, can be refrozen and used multiple times, offering a cost-effective and environmentally friendly option for prolonged cooling needs, such as transporting perishable goods or maintaining food temperatures during picnics.</p></p>
