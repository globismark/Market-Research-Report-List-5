<p><p>The lithography steppers market is categorized into several types based on their light sources and applications. UV (Ultraviolet) lithography is commonly used for standard semiconductor processes. DUV (Deep Ultraviolet) offers higher resolution for advanced chips. EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) enables the fabrication of nanoscale structures essential for cutting-edge technology. Nanoimprint lithography is a niche technique that creates patterns through mechanical processes, suitable for emerging applications like nanotechnology. Each type plays a critical role in advancing semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
