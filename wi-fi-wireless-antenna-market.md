<p><p>The Wi-Fi wireless antenna market includes two primary types: omni-directional antennas and directional antennas. Omni-directional antennas emit signals in a 360-degree pattern, making them ideal for broader coverage in open areas, such as homes and offices. In contrast, directional antennas focus signals in a specific direction, providing increased range and connectivity for targeted applications, such as point-to-point links or long-distance connections. Both types cater to varied needs, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of wireless networks.</p></p>
