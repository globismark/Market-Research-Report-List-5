<p><p>The Automotive Seatbelt Retractor market consists of three main types. Automatic Locking Retractors secure the seatbelt when the occupant is seated, allowing for adjustment but locking upon sudden vehicle movement. Emergency Locking Retractors activate during abrupt stops or collisions, tightening the belt to keep the occupant safe. Switchable Retractors offer flexibility, allowing users to switch between automatic and emergency modes, catering to different seating needs. Each type enhances passenger safety and comfort through varied locking mechanisms.</p></p>
