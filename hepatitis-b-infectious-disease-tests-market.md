Antibody testing is used to detect the presence of antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the hepatitis B virus. Antigen testing involves identifying specific proteins produced by the virus. Nucleic acid testing detects the genetic material (DNA or RNA) of the virus. Each type of test plays a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring hepatitis B infection.</p></p>
