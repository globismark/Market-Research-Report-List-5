<p><p>The implantable cardiac stimulators market includes four primary types: </p><p>1. **Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillator (ICD)**: Devices that monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks to correct life-threatening arrhythmias.</p><p>2. **Pacemakers**: Small devices that regulate slow heartbeats by providing electrical stimulation.</p><p>3. **Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillator (CRT-D)**: Combines pacemaker and defibrillator functions to improve heart efficiency in patients with heart failure.</p><p>4. **Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Pacemaker (CRT-P)**: Similar to CRT-D but without defibrillator capabilities, used primarily to manage heart failure symptoms.</p></p>
