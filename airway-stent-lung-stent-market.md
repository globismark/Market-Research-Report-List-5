<p><p>The airway stent market comprises three main types: metal stents, silicone stents, and hybrid stents. Metal stents, often made from stainless steel or nitinol, provide strong structural support and are used in more complex airway obstructions. Silicone stents are flexible, biocompatible, and suitable for short-term applications, minimizing tissue irritation. Hybrid stents combine features of both metal and silicone, offering flexibility and durability for varying treatment needs. These stents are essential in managing airway conditions and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
