<p><p>The Performance Monitoring Module market encompasses various types of testing equipment, including Optical Power Meters (OPM), Optical Time-Domain Reflectometers (OTDR), and Optical Loss Test Sets (OLTS). OPM measures the power levels in optical fibers to ensure signal integrity. OTDR analyzes the time and intensity of light reflections to identify faults and measure distances within the fiber. OLTS assesses overall fiber loss in a network by testing the optical power transmitted and received, ensuring optimal performance and reliability in communication systems.</p></p>
