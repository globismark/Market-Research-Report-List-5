<p><p>The Industrial Coating Additives Market encompasses various types that enhance coating performance. Rheology modifiers control the viscosity and flow properties, ensuring ease of application. Defoamers reduce and prevent foam during production, improving texture and finish. Dispersants facilitate uniform distribution of pigments and fillers, enhancing stability and color consistency. Wetting agents improve surface interaction, promoting better adhesion and coverage. Other additives may include biocides and surfactants, each contributing to specific functionalities for optimized coating formulations.</p></p>
