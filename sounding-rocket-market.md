<p><p>Sounding Rocket Market includes two main types: One Stage Sounding Rocket and Multistage Sounding Rocket. One Stage Sounding Rockets are simpler in design and consist of a single rocket stage that carries the payload to a certain altitude before descending back to Earth. Multistage Sounding Rockets, on the other hand, consist of two or more stages that ignite in succession to achieve greater altitude and speed. They are capable of carrying heavier payloads and reaching higher altitudes compared to one stage rockets.</p></p>
