<p><p>Minimally Invasive Vertebral Compression Fracture Repair market includes Vertebroplasty Devices and Kyphoplasty Devices. Vertebroplasty Devices are used to stabilize vertebrae by injecting bone cement into the fractured bone, providing immediate pain relief. Kyphoplasty Devices involve the use of a balloon to create a void in the fractured bone before filling it with bone cement, helping to restore vertebral height and reduce deformity. Both devices offer minimally invasive options for treating vertebral compression fractures, improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
