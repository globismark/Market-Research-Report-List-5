<p><p>Glass fibre filters and prefilters are essential components in air filtration systems, catering to various standards like ASHRAE, HEPA, and ULPA. ASHRAE filters capture larger particles, enhancing general air quality. HEPA filters provide high efficiency by trapping 99.97% of particles down to 0.3 microns, making them ideal for medical and cleanroom applications. ULPA filters offer even higher filtration efficiency, capturing 99.999% of particles, crucial for environments requiring stringent cleanliness standards, such as pharmaceutical manufacturing and laboratories.</p></p>
