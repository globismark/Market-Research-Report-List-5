<p><p>The lung function testers market encompasses various devices used to assess respiratory health. Spirometers measure airflow and lung volumes, while breath CO monitors gauge carbon monoxide levels in exhaled air, indicating smoking or air quality exposure. Oscillometers evaluate respiratory resistance and compliance through oscillation frequency. Ergospirometers assess exercise capacity by measuring oxygen uptake during physical activity. Dose-controlled drug nebulizers deliver medication directly to the lungs in precise doses. Other devices may include peak flow meters and advanced imaging technologies for comprehensive lung assessment.</p></p>
