<p><p>The sunflower oilseed processing market is primarily divided into mechanical and chemical processing types. Mechanical processing involves methods like cold pressing, where sunflower seeds are crushed to extract oil without additional chemicals, preserving flavor and nutrients. In contrast, chemical processing uses solvents, typically hexane, to efficiently extract oil from seeds, allowing for higher yields but potentially altering the oil's characteristics. Each method caters to different consumer preferences and industry requirements, influencing product quality and market demand.</p></p>
