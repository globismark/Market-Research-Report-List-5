<p><p>Diffusion furnaces are essential in the solar cell manufacturing process, primarily for phosphorus and boron doping of silicon wafers. The two main types are horizontal and vertical furnaces. Horizontal furnaces enable uniform temperature distribution and accommodate larger batches, making them suitable for high-volume production. In contrast, vertical furnaces are more space-efficient, offering quicker thermal cycling and precise control for small-batch applications. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and quality of solar cells in the market.</p></p>
