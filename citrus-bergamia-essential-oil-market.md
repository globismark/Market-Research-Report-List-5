<p><p>Citrus Bergamia Essential Oil is available in two main types: Photosensitive Bergamot and Non-Photosensitive Bergamot. Photosensitive Bergamot is known for its ability to react negatively when exposed to sunlight, causing skin irritation and burns. As a result, this type of Bergamot is not recommended for use in products that will be applied to skin that will be exposed to sunlight. On the other hand, Non-Photosensitive Bergamot does not have this same reaction to sunlight, making it a safer option for use in skincare products and perfumes.</p></p>
