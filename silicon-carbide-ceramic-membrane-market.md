<p><p>The Silicon Carbide Ceramic Membrane market is divided into two main types: flat ceramic membranes and tubular ceramic membranes. Flat ceramic membranes typically offer a larger surface area for filtration, making them suitable for various applications like water treatment and chemical processing. In contrast, tubular ceramic membranes are designed in a cylindrical shape, providing high flow rates and greater resistance to fouling, ideal for demanding processing environments. Both types leverage the excellent durability and thermal stability of silicon carbide for enhanced performance.</p></p>
