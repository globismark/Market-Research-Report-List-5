<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive</li><li>Capacitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure sensor dies market comprises mainly two types: piezoresistive and capacitive. Piezoresistive sensors detect pressure changes through resistance variations in a conductive material, offering high sensitivity and accuracy, typically used in harsh environments. Capacitive sensors, on the other hand, measure pressure by detecting changes in capacitance between conductive plates, providing excellent stability and linearity, ideal for applications requiring precise measurements. Both types are vital in automotive, industrial, and medical sectors, addressing diverse pressure measurement needs.</p></p>
