Each type offers unique characteristics such as exceptional chemical resistance, high temperature resistance, low friction, and non-stick properties. Other fluoropolymer coatings in the market may include specialty formulations tailored for specific applications or industries, offering customized properties to meet varying requirements.</p></p>
