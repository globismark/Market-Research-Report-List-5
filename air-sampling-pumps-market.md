<p><p>Air sampling pumps are essential for collecting air contaminants for analysis, and they are categorized into high flow and low flow types. High flow pumps operate at a greater volume, making them suitable for sampling large areas or during intensive monitoring situations, often used in industrial applications. In contrast, low flow pumps are designed for precision and are ideal for personal monitoring or environments where lower airflow rates are necessary to capture specific airborne particles or gases accurately.</p></p>
