<p><strong>The Gelatin Capsule Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stomach-soluble Gelatin Capsules</li><li>Enterosoluble Vacant Gelatin Capsules</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gelatin capsule market comprises several types based on their solubility properties. Stomach-soluble gelatin capsules dissolve in the acidic environment of the stomach, ensuring rapid release of their contents. Enterosoluble vacant gelatin capsules, on the other hand, are designed to withstand stomach acidity, dissolving instead in the more alkaline environment of the intestines for targeted release. Other market types may include specialized formulations or variations designed for specific applications, enhancing the versatility and functionality of gelatin capsules in pharmaceutical and nutritional products.</p></p>
