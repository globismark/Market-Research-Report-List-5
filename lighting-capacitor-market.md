<p><strong>The Lighting Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type Lighting Capacitor</li><li>Oil Filled Type Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lighting capacitor market comprises primarily two types: dry type and oil-filled type capacitors. Dry type lighting capacitors use a solid dielectric material, offering advantages like compact size and reduced maintenance, making them ideal for indoor applications. In contrast, oil-filled capacitors utilize liquid insulation, which allows for higher voltage ratings and better thermal management, suitable for outdoor and high-power applications. Both types are crucial for enhancing energy efficiency and supporting voltage regulation in lighting systems.</p></p>
