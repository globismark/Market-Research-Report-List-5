<p><p>The Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) system market encompasses various technologies for automated toll collection. RFID-based systems utilize radio-frequency identification for seamless vehicle recognition. DSRC (Dedicated Short Range Communications)-based systems employ short-range wireless communication for real-time data exchange. Video analytic-based systems leverage cameras and software to assess vehicle movement and automatically calculate tolls. GNSS/GPS-based systems rely on satellite positioning to determine vehicle location for toll assessment, offering a flexible and scalable solution for toll collection across diverse infrastructures.</p></p>
