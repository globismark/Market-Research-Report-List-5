<p><p>The ambulatory cardiac monitoring market comprises several types, including Holter monitors, event monitors, mobile cardiac telemetry, and other monitoring devices. Holter monitors provide continuous ECG recording for 24-48 hours, capturing heart rhythms during daily activities. Event monitors record heart activity during symptomatic episodes, activated by the patient. Mobile cardiac telemetry offers real-time remote monitoring and automatic data transmission, enabling immediate analysis of heart conditions. Other devices in this market may include wearable technology and implantable monitors, enhancing patient care and diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
