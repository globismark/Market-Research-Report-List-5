<p><p>Automotive refinish paint market consists of three main types: primer & base coat, top coat, and clear coat. The primer & base coat is applied first to provide a smooth surface and enhance adhesion. The top coat provides the desired color and finish to the vehicle. The clear coat is then applied as a final protective layer to provide gloss and durability. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring a high-quality and long-lasting paint job for automotive vehicles.</p></p>
