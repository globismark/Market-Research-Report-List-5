Adhesive bonding involves using special adhesives to join pipes together. Welding, on the other hand, involves melting the edges of the pipes and fusing them together. Each method has its advantages and applications depending on the specific needs of the project. The market for plastic pipe jointing and welding is driven by the demand for reliable and durable connections in various industries.</p></p>
