<p><strong>The Farm Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The farm tire market is primarily divided into two categories: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature overlapping layers of fabric, offering strength and durability, making them suitable for rough terrains and heavy loads. They provide excellent traction but have a stiffer ride. Conversely, radial tires consist of layers arranged radially, enhancing flexibility and grip, which leads to better fuel efficiency and a smoother ride. Each type serves specific agricultural needs, balancing performance, cost, and application requirements.</p></p>
