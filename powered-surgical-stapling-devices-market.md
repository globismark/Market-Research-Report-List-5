<p><p>Powered surgical stapling devices are categorized into three main types: straight, curved, and circular. Straight staplers are primarily used for linear incision closures in various surgical procedures. Curved staplers are designed for applications where access is limited, enabling better maneuverability around anatomical structures. Circular staplers are ideal for creating anastomoses, particularly in gastrointestinal surgeries, providing a secure connection between tubular structures. Each type is tailored to specific surgical needs, enhancing efficiency and precision during procedures.</p></p>
