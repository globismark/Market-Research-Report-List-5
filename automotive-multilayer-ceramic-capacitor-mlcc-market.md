X5R types provide lower capacitance with moderate stability, suitable for value-sensitive applications. C0G (NP0) capacitors exhibit high stability and low loss, perfect for precision circuits. Y5V capacitors offer high capacitance but with less stability, suitable for non-critical applications. Other types include specialized variants for niche automotive needs.</p></p>
