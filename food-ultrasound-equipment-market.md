<p><p>The food ultrasound equipment market is categorized into two main types: high-frequency low-intensity and low-frequency high-intensity ultrasound. High-frequency low-intensity ultrasound is primarily used for non-destructive testing, enhancing food quality, and improving safety by identifying contaminants without altering the food itself. In contrast, low-frequency high-intensity ultrasound is utilized for processes such as emulsification, extraction, and homogenization, facilitating food processing and enhancing texture and flavor. Both types serve distinct purposes in food technology, optimizing production methods and quality.</p></p>
