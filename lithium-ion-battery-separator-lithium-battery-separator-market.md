<p><p>The Lithium-Ion Battery Separator market is primarily divided into two types: bilayer and trilayer separators. Bilayer lithium-ion battery separators consist of two distinct layers that enhance ionic conductivity while providing mechanical strength and thermal stability. In contrast, trilayer separators incorporate an additional layer, offering improved safety features, reduced risk of thermal runaway, and better electrochemical performance. These innovations aim to optimize battery efficiency and lifespan, catering to the growing demands of electric vehicles and portable electronic devices.</p></p>
