<p><strong>The Analog Excitation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Brushless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Analog Excitation System market is primarily divided into two types: Static and Brushless systems. Static excitation systems use thyristors to control voltage without moving parts, offering high reliability and low maintenance. In contrast, Brushless excitation systems utilize rotating components, eliminating brushes that can wear out, which enhances durability and efficiency. Both types are essential in maintaining voltage stability in power generation, but their operational mechanisms cater to different needs and preferences in industrial applications.</p></p>
