<p><p>The navigation lights market comprises three primary types: LED lamps, halogen lamps, and xenon lamps. LED lamps are favored for their energy efficiency, longevity, and low heat emission, making them highly suitable for modern vessels. Halogen lamps produce bright light and have a shorter lifespan but are known for their warm illumination. Xenon lamps offer intense brightness and faster start-up times, often used in high-performance applications. Each type caters to specific preferences and vessel requirements in marine navigation.</p></p>
