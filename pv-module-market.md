Ground-mounted systems are typically large-scale installations on open land, ideal for utility-scale projects. Pole-mounted systems elevate solar panels on poles, maximizing space and improving efficiency, commonly used in rural or limited areas. Rooftop solar installations are mounted on residential or commercial buildings, allowing users to generate electricity on-site and reduce reliance on the grid, promoting energy independence and sustainability.</p></p>
