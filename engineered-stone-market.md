<p><strong>The Engineered Stone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Surface</li><li>Engineered Quartz Stone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineered stone market primarily consists of two main types: solid surface and engineered quartz stone. Solid surface materials are created from acrylic or polyester resins combined with natural minerals, offering a seamless, customizable aesthetic for countertops and sinks. Engineered quartz stone, on the other hand, is composed of about 90% crushed natural quartz combined with resins and polymers, providing durability and a wide range of colors and patterns. Both types are popular for their versatility, durability, and low maintenance requirements.</p></p>
