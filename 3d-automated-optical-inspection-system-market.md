<p><p>The 3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) System market is primarily divided into inline and offline types. Inline 3D AOI systems are integrated directly into the production line, enabling real-time inspection and immediate feedback, which enhances efficiency and reduces defects during manufacturing. Offline 3D AOI systems, on the other hand, operate separately from the production line, allowing for detailed inspections after assembly. This type is ideal for thorough quality checks and rework processes, ensuring high-quality standards post-production.</p></p>
