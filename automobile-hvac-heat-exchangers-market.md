<p><p>The automobile HVAC heat exchangers market consists of three main types: condensers, heat cores, and evaporators. Condensers cool and condense refrigerant, releasing heat to the outside environment. Heat cores, often used for cabin heating, transfer engine coolant heat to the interior. Evaporators facilitate cooling by absorbing heat from the cabin air, allowing refrigerant to evaporate. Together, these components enhance passenger comfort by regulating temperature and ensuring efficient climate control within vehicles.</p></p>
