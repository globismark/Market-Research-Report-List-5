<p><p>Solvent-borne adhesives are classified into different types based on the type of polymers used in their formulation. Some common types include Chloroprene Rubber adhesives, which are known for their high strength and durability. Poly Acrylate (PA) adhesives offer excellent heat resistance and flexibility. Styrenic Block Copolymer (SBC) Resin adhesives are known for their high adhesive strength and versatility. Other types of solvent-borne adhesives may include polyvinyl acetate (PVA) adhesives, epoxy adhesives, and polyurethane adhesives, each offering unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
