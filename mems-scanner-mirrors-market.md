<p><p>The MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) scanner mirrors market is primarily segmented into two types: mono-axial and dual-axial scanner mirrors. Mono-axial scanner mirrors operate along a single axis, making them suitable for applications like optical beam steering in projectors and LIDAR systems. In contrast, dual-axial scanner mirrors can tilt along two axes, providing enhanced versatility and precision for applications such as rangefinders and advanced imaging systems. Both types serve diverse sectors, including consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
