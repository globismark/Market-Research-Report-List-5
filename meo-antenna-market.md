<p><p>The MEO (Medium Earth Orbit) antenna market can be categorized into two primary types: active and passive antennas. Active antennas incorporate electronic components that amplify signals, providing enhanced performance and adaptability for communication systems. They are ideal for dynamic environments requiring real-time adjustments. In contrast, passive antennas rely solely on their physical structure to receive and transmit signals, offering a simpler, often more cost-effective solution. Both types play crucial roles in satellite communications and data transmission systems.</p></p>
