<p><p>The electric bus and coach market encompasses several types. Full Battery Electric Buses (BEB) run solely on electric power stored in batteries, offering zero emissions and lower operational costs. Hybrid Electric Buses (HEB) combine conventional engines with electric propulsion, improving fuel efficiency and reducing emissions while maintaining extended range. Fuel Cell Electric Buses (FCEB) utilize hydrogen fuel cells to generate electricity, emitting only water vapor and providing longer range options, making them suitable for various urban and intercity transport applications.</p></p>
