<p><p>Ready Mixed Concrete (RMX) market comprises of three main types: Transit Mixed Concrete, Shrink Mixed Concrete, and Central Mixed Concrete. Transit Mixed Concrete involves mixing concrete in a truck while in transit to the construction site. Shrink Mixed Concrete is partially mixed at the plant and then completed in a mixer truck at the site. Central Mixed Concrete is fully mixed at a central plant and then transported to the site in a truck. Each type offers varying levels of convenience and quality control for construction projects.</p></p>
