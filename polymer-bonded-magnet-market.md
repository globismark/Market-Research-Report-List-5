<p><ul><li>Injection magnet</li><li>Pressed magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polymer Bonded Magnet market consists primarily of two types: injection magnets and pressed magnets. Injection magnets are produced by injecting polymer-based materials containing magnetic powders into molds, allowing for complex shapes and high precision. Pressed magnets, on the other hand, involve compacting the material into a die under high pressure and then sintering it, resulting in strong, dense magnets. Each type caters to specific applications, offering advantages in terms of cost, customization, and magnetic performance.</p></p>
