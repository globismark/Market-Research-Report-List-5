<p><strong>The Pneumatic Structural Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Supported Structures</li><li>Air-Inflated Structures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pneumatic Structural Systems market includes Air-Supported and Air-Inflated Structures, which utilize air pressure for stability and shape. Air-Supported Structures rely on continuous air pressure to maintain their form, often seen in temporary buildings and sports facilities. In contrast, Air-Inflated Structures use sealed membranes that can be inflated to create rigid forms. Both types offer lightweight, flexible, and energy-efficient solutions for various applications, including warehouses, event spaces, and recreational facilities, with increasing popularity due to their rapid deployment and sustainability features.</p></p>
