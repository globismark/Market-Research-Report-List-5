<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 antibody detection kits market comprises several types, including Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick results, often within minutes, suitable for field use. ELISA is a laboratory-based method that offers high sensitivity and specificity, making it ideal for clinical settings. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to inhibit viral infection, providing valuable insights into immunity levels. Each type serves distinct roles in diagnosing and understanding COVID-19 immunity.</p></p>
