<p><p>The garlic peeling machines market is segmented into two main types: wet-type and dry-type. Wet-type machines utilize water to loosen and remove the garlic skins, ensuring a gentle peeling process that preserves the cloves. Conversely, dry-type machines rely on mechanical methods, often utilizing friction or air pressure to achieve skin removal without moisture. Each type serves various industry needs, with wet-type favored for smaller batches and dry-type ideal for high-volume processing environments, offering efficiency and speed.</p></p>
