<p><p>Bi-axially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) is a versatile plastic film widely used in packaging due to its excellent clarity, strength, and chemical resistance. The market includes two primary manufacturing methods: the Flat Film Strength Method and the Tubular Quench Method. The Flat Film method produces film by stretching polypropylene in two directions, resulting in higher tensile strength and durability. Conversely, the Tubular Quench Method creates a continuous tube, offering efficient production and versatility in packaging applications, catering to varying market demands.</p></p>
