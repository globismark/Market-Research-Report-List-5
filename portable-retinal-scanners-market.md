<p><p>The portable retinal scanners market is divided into two main types: no mydriasis and mydriasis. No mydriasis scanners operate without the need to dilate the pupil, allowing for quick and convenient assessments, making them suitable for primary care and screening settings. In contrast, mydriasis scanners require pupil dilation for optimal imaging, providing enhanced detail for diagnostic purposes. Both types cater to different clinical needs, enhancing accessibility and efficiency in retinal examinations.</p></p>
