<p><p>The nitrocellulose membranes market is categorized based on pore sizes, which influence their applications in various fields. Membranes with pore sizes below 0.45 µm are typically used for fine filtration, retaining small particles and microorganisms, making them ideal for medical and laboratory settings. Pore sizes ranging from 0.45 to 6 µm are suitable for a broader range of filtration tasks, including water purification and sample preparation. Membranes with pore sizes above 6 µm are generally utilized for coarse filtration applications, such as pre-filtration processes.</p></p>
