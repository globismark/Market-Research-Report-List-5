<p><p>Reaction torque sensors are essential devices that measure torque applied to a system. The market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave)** sensors utilize acoustic wave technology for high precision in torque measurement.</p><p>2. **Optical sensors** deploy light signals to detect changes in torque, offering high sensitivity and accuracy.</p><p>3. **Strain gauge sensors** measure the strain on materials to calculate torque, being widely used due to their cost-effectiveness.</p><p>4. **Magnetoelastic sensors** exploit changes in magnetic properties under stress, providing robust measurements in diverse applications.</p></p>
