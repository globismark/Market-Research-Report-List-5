<p><p>The retroreflective material market is segmented into two key types: traffic safety systems and personal protective equipment (PPE). Traffic safety systems utilize retroreflective materials in road signs, markings, and vehicle registration plates to enhance visibility and improve safety on roadways, especially at night or in low-light conditions. In contrast, personal protective equipment incorporates retroreflective materials into garments like vests and uniforms, ensuring individuals working in hazardous environments, such as construction or emergency services, are easily seen, reducing accident risks.</p></p>
