<p><ul><li>Wet Sprayed Concrete</li><li>Dry Sprayed Concrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sprayed concrete market consists of two primary types: wet sprayed concrete and dry sprayed concrete. Wet sprayed concrete involves a pre-mixed mixture of cement, water, and aggregates that is pumped and sprayed onto surfaces, ensuring a dense and cohesive application. In contrast, dry sprayed concrete utilizes a dry mixture of cement and aggregates, which is conveyed through a hose and mixed with water at the nozzle. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of application techniques, curing times, and project requirements.</p></p>
