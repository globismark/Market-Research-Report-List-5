<p><p>The plasma surface treatment equipment market is divided into two main types: low pressure/vacuum plasma and atmospheric plasma treatment equipment. Low pressure/vacuum plasma systems operate in controlled environments, allowing precise treatment of surfaces to enhance adhesion, cleanliness, and chemical properties. In contrast, atmospheric plasma equipment functions at atmospheric pressure, enabling more flexible and rapid processing of materials without the need for vacuum chambers. Both types improve surface characteristics for applications across various industries, including automotive, electronics, and medical devices.</p></p>
