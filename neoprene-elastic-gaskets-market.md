Saddle gaskets have a U-shaped configuration that offers enhanced sealing capabilities, making them ideal for irregular surfaces and dynamic applications. In contrast, waveform gaskets possess a wavy profile that facilitates better compression and adaptability, ensuring a secure seal even under varying pressure conditions. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive and manufacturing, providing durability and chemical resistance essential for reliable operations.</p></p>
