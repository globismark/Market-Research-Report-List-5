<p><p>The polyethylene packaging market encompasses various types, including High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), and Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE). HDPE is known for its strength and rigidity, making it ideal for containers and bottles. LDPE offers flexibility and is commonly used in plastic bags and films. LLDPE combines the attributes of both HDPE and LDPE, providing increased tensile strength and elasticity, suitable for stretch films and durable packaging solutions. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance characteristics.</p></p>
