<p><strong>The Piezoresistive Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diffuse Silicon Piezoresistive</li><li>Ceramic Piezoresistive</li><li>Strain Gage Piezoresistive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoresistive pressure sensors are categorized into three main types. Diffuse silicon piezoresistive sensors utilize silicon wafers to create a sensitive element, offering high accuracy and stability for various applications. Ceramic piezoresistive sensors are made from durable ceramic materials, providing excellent resistance to harsh environments and high temperature. Strain gauge piezoresistive sensors employ a strain gauge attached to a diaphragm, translating pressure changes into electrical signals, ideal for applications requiring precise measurements in industrial settings.</p></p>
