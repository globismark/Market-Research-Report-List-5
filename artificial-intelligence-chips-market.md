<p><p>The artificial intelligence chips market encompasses various types of processors designed for AI tasks. GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) excel in parallel processing, making them ideal for training complex models. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) are custom-designed for specific AI applications, offering high efficiency. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) provide flexibility, allowing reconfiguration for different tasks while maintaining performance. CPUs (Central Processing Units) serve as general-purpose processors, handling a range of tasks but typically with less efficiency in dedicated AI operations compared to other types.</p></p>
