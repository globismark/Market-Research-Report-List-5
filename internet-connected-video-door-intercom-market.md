<p><p>The Internet Connected Video Door Intercom market is categorized into several types, including Wi-Fi Type and Radio Frequency Type. Wi-Fi Type intercoms utilize existing wireless internet networks, enabling easy installation and remote access via smartphones. They offer high-definition video and audio quality. Radio Frequency Type intercoms, on the other hand, operate independently of Wi-Fi networks, using dedicated radio signals for communication. This type is often more reliable in areas with poor internet connectivity but may have limited features compared to Wi-Fi models.</p></p>
