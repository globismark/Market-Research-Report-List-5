<p><p>The unmanned VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) crafts market can be categorized into two main types: fixed-wing and rotary. Fixed-wing VTOL crafts combine the efficiency of traditional airplanes with vertical takeoff capabilities, making them ideal for long-range missions while maintaining fuel efficiency. In contrast, rotary VTOL crafts utilize rotor blades for vertical lift, offering greater maneuverability and the ability to operate in confined spaces. Both types are increasingly utilized in various sectors, including logistics, surveillance, and agriculture.</p></p>
