<p><p>Surgical cautery pens are available in two main market types: hand control and foot control. Hand control cautery pens are operated by the surgeon holding the device in their hand, allowing for precise control and maneuverability. On the other hand, foot control cautery pens are operated by pressing foot pedals, allowing the surgeon to have hands-free control during procedures. Both types of cautery pens offer benefits in different surgical settings and cater to the preferences and needs of surgeons.</p></p>
