<p><p>The dash cam market is primarily divided into two types: single-channel and multi-channel dash cameras. Single-channel dash cameras typically focus on capturing footage from the front of the vehicle, providing essential evidence in case of accidents. In contrast, multi-channel dash cameras feature multiple cameras to record various angles, including front, rear, and interior views, enhancing overall safety and security. This differentiation caters to diverse consumer needs, from basic monitoring to comprehensive surveillance for personal and commercial vehicles.</p></p>
