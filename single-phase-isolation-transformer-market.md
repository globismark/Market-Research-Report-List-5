<p><p>The single-phase isolation transformer market is divided into two main types: step-up transformers and step-down transformers. Step-up transformers increase voltage from a lower level to a higher level, making them suitable for applications requiring voltage elevation to meet equipment specifications or transmission needs. Conversely, step-down transformers reduce voltage from a higher level to a lower level, essential for connecting high-voltage systems to lower-voltage equipment. Both types are crucial for electrical safety, efficiency, and system compatibility in various industries.</p></p>
