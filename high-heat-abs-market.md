<p><p>The High Heat ABS market is divided into two primary segments: High Heat Resistant and Super High Heat Resistant materials. High Heat Resistant ABS can withstand elevated temperatures, typically up to 100°C, making it suitable for automotive and electrical applications. In contrast, Super High Heat Resistant ABS offers superior thermal stability and can endure temperatures exceeding 100°C, making it ideal for demanding environments like aerospace and specialized electronics. Both types cater to industries requiring durability and reliability under heat exposure.</p></p>
