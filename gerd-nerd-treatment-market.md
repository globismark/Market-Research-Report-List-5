<p><p>GERD & NERD treatment market includes three main types of medications: Antacids (Acid Neutralizers), Proton Pump Inhibitors, and H2 Receptor Blockers. Antacids work by neutralizing stomach acid, providing quick relief. Proton Pump Inhibitors reduce the production of acid in the stomach, providing longer-lasting relief. H2 Receptor Blockers also reduce stomach acid production by blocking histamine receptors. Each type of medication has its own benefits, and the choice of treatment is often based on the severity of the condition and individual patient needs.</p></p>
