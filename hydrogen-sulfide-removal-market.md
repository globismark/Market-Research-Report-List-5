<p><p>The Hydrogen Sulfide (H2S) removal market is divided into two main types: regenerative and non-regenerative systems. Non-regenerative systems, on the other hand, involve consumable materials that need to be replaced after saturation. While regenerative systems offer sustainability, non-regenerative methods often provide simpler, one-time solutions for applications with lower H2S concentrations or shorter operational needs.</p></p>
