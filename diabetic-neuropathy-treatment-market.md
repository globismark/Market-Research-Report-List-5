<p><p>Diabetic Neuropathy Treatment Market consists of different types based on the location and symptoms of the nerve damage. Peripheral Neuropathy affects the hands and feet, causing numbness and pain. Autonomic Neuropathy affects the involuntary nervous system, leading to digestive issues, heart problems, and sexual dysfunction. Proximal Neuropathy affects the thighs, hips, or buttocks, causing weakness and pain. Focal Neuropathy results in sudden, severe pain in a specific nerve or group of nerves. Different treatment approaches are used for each type of diabetic neuropathy.</p></p>
