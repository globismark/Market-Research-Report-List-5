<p><strong>The NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic, Radiographic</li><li>Eddy Current</li><li>Magnetic particle</li><li>Acoustic Emission</li><li>Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services market includes various methods for evaluating materials and structures without causing damage. Ultrasonic testing uses high-frequency sound waves to detect flaws. Radiographic testing utilizes X-rays or gamma rays to view internal features. Eddy current testing measures electrical conductivity to identify defects. Magnetic particle testing reveals surface defects through magnetic fields. Acoustic emission monitors stress waves for real-time defect detection. Terahertz imaging employs terahertz radiation for detailed imaging of materials, enhancing inspection capabilities across industries.</p></p>
