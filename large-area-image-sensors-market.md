<p><strong>The Large-Area Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS Image Sensor</li><li>CCD Image Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large-area image sensors market primarily comprises two types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) image sensors. CMOS sensors are favored for their low power consumption, faster readout capabilities, and integration with digital circuits, making them ideal for applications in smartphones and security systems. In contrast, CCD sensors are known for their superior image quality and sensitivity, often used in scientific and medical imaging. The choice between them depends on specific application needs such as speed, power, and image fidelity.</p></p>
