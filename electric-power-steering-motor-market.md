<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Steering (EPS) motors come in various types including Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS) motor, Rack Electric Power Steering (REPS) motor, Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS) motor, and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) motor. CEPS motor is mounted directly onto the steering column, REPS motor is mounted on the steering rack, PEPS motor is mounted on the steering pinion, and EHPS motor combines electric and hydraulic power to assist steering. Each type offers unique advantages and features for vehicles with electric power steering systems.</p></p>
