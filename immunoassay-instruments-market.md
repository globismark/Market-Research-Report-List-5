<p><strong>The Immunoassay Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li><li>Immunofluorescence Analyzers</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li><li>Elisa</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunoassay instruments market includes various techniques for detecting and quantifying biomarkers. Chemiluminescence immunoassays use light emission for sensitivity and speed. Immunofluorescence analyzers utilize fluorescent tags to visualize antigens, allowing precise localization. Radioimmunoassay employs radioactive isotopes for detection, offering high specificity but limited due to safety concerns. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is widely used for its simplicity and versatility, involving enzyme-substrate reactions to produce measurable signals. Each method serves diverse diagnostic applications and impacts clinical decision-making.</p></p>
