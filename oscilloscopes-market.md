<p><ul><li>Analog Oscilloscope</li><li>Digital Oscilloscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog oscilloscopes use a cathode ray tube to display waveforms in real-time, providing a continuous trace of the signal. They are generally preferred for their ability to show subtle details of a waveform. On the other hand, digital oscilloscopes convert analog signals into digital data for display on a screen. They offer more advanced features such as signal analysis, storage, and the ability to save and export data. Each type of oscilloscope caters to different user needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
