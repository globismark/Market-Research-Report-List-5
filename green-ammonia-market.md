<p><strong>The Green Ammonia Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkaline Water Electrolysis</li><li>Proton Exchange Membrane</li><li>Solid Oxide Electrolysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The green ammonia market encompasses three primary electrolysis technologies: alkaline water electrolysis, proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolysis, and solid oxide electrolysis. Alkaline water electrolysis uses a liquid alkaline electrolyte, known for its cost-effectiveness but lower efficiency. PEM electrolysis employs a solid polymer electrolyte, offering higher efficiency and quick response times, suitable for variable renewable energy. Solid oxide electrolysis operates at high temperatures, achieving high efficiency and lower energy consumption, making it ideal for large-scale hydrogen production from renewable sources.</p></p>
