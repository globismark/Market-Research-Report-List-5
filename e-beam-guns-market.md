<p><p>E-Beam guns are essential in various industrial applications, primarily for vacuum deposition processes. The market is segmented into two types: Multi-Pocket E-Beam Guns and Single-Pocket E-Beam Guns. Multi-Pocket E-Beam Guns feature multiple evaporation sources, allowing simultaneous processing of different materials for enhanced efficiency and throughput. In contrast, Single-Pocket E-Beam Guns provide a simpler setup for specific applications, focusing on one material at a time, which is ideal for smaller production runs or specialized processes.</p></p>
