<p><p>Cast lines for stretch film production are categorized into two main types: multi-layer and single-layer. Multi-layer cast lines facilitate the production of stretch films that combine different resin types, enhancing properties such as strength, clarity, and barrier performance. This results in films suitable for varied applications. In contrast, single-layer cast lines produce films from a single type of resin, typically offering basic performance and cost-effectiveness. Each type serves distinct market needs, balancing performance and production efficiency.</p></p>
