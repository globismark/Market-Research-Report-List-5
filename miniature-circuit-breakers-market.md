<p><p>Miniature-Circuit Breakers (MCBs) are categorized by their trip characteristics, which are classified into three main types: B, C, and D. B Type MCBs are designed for residential and light commercial applications, with a trip curve that responds to moderate overloads. C Type MCBs are suitable for general commercial and industrial applications, with a trip curve that responds to high inrush currents. D Type MCBs are designed for very specific applications, such as motor protection or heavy industrial machinery, with a trip curve that responds to very high inrush currents.</p></p>
