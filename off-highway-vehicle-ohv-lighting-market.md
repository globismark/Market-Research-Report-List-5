<p><p>The Off Highway Vehicle (OHV) lighting market encompasses various technologies, including LED, halogen, HID, and incandescent lighting. LED lighting is energy-efficient, durable, and offers high brightness, making it popular for versatility and longevity. Halogen lighting provides strong illumination at a lower upfront cost but has a shorter lifespan. HID lighting delivers intense brightness and range, ideal for off-road applications, though it requires a ballast. Incandescent lighting, while less common due to lower efficiency, offers warm light but consumes more energy and burns out faster.</p></p>
