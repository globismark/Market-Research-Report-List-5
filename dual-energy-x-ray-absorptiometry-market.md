<p><p>The Dual Energy X-ray Absorptiometry (DXA) market includes two primary types: Axial Bone Densitometers and Peripheral Bone Densitometers. Axial Bone Densitometers are designed for evaluating the bone mineral density of critical areas such as the spine and hip, making them suitable for diagnosing osteoporosis. In contrast, Peripheral Bone Densitometers assess bone density in peripheral sites like the wrist and heel, offering a portable and cost-effective option for screenings. Both types contribute to effective bone health management and disease prevention.</p></p>
