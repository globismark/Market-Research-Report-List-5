<p><ul><li>Single Wheel Blower</li><li>Double Wheel Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive blowers market can be categorized into Single Wheel and Double Wheel Blowers. Single Wheel Blowers feature a single impeller, ideal for compact applications requiring moderate airflow and pressure. They are commonly used in basic heating and air conditioning systems. In contrast, Double Wheel Blowers utilize two impellers, allowing for increased airflow and efficiency, making them suitable for larger vehicles or applications needing higher performance. Both types play essential roles in climate control and ventilation systems within vehicles.</p></p>
