<p><ul><li>Soy Protein Concentrates</li><li>Soy Protein Isolates</li><li>Soy Protein Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic soy protein market comprises three main types: soy protein concentrates, soy protein isolates, and soy protein flour. Soy protein concentrates retain a majority of the protein content while removing some fats and carbohydrates, making them suitable for various food applications. Soy protein isolates provide the highest protein content and minimal fats, ideal for protein supplements and fortified products. Soy protein flour, containing protein and fiber, is used primarily in baking and as a meat extender, offering nutritional benefits and functional properties.</p></p>
