<p><strong>The Industrial Alginate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High G</li><li>Low G</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial alginate market is categorized into High G and Low G types based on the molecular composition of alginic acid. High G alginates, derived primarily from brown seaweed species, possess higher viscosity and form strong gels, making them ideal for applications in food processing, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. Low G alginates, on the other hand, offer lower viscosity and flexibility, making them suitable for uses in textile and paper industries. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, driven by their unique properties.</p></p>
