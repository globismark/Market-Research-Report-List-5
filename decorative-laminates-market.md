<p><ul><li>High-pressure Laminate</li><li>Low-pressure Laminate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Decorative Laminates market comprises two primary types: High-pressure Laminate (HPL) and Low-pressure Laminate (LPL). HPL is created through a process involving heat and pressure, resulting in a durable, scratch-resistant surface ideal for high-traffic areas, often used in countertops and furniture. Conversely, LPL involves a less rigorous manufacturing process, making it more cost-effective, yet suitable for applications like wall panels and cabinetry. Both types offer aesthetic appeal and protective qualities for various interior design projects.</p></p>
