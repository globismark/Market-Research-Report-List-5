<p><p>The metallic label market is primarily segmented into two main types: cold foil metallic labels and hot foil metallic labels. Cold foil metallic labels utilize a cold foil transfer process, allowing intricate designs with vibrant metallic finishes without the need for heat. In contrast, hot foil metallic labels involve applying heat to foil materials, enabling bold and striking metallic effects on labels. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing product aesthetics and brand visibility through their unique visual appeal.</p></p>
