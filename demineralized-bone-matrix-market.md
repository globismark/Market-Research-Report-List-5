<p><p>Demineralized Bone Matrix (DBM) is a biocompatible grafting material derived from human or animal bone that has undergone a demineralization process, removing the mineral content while preserving the organic matrix. This transformation enhances its osteoinductive properties, making it valuable for various orthopedic and dental applications, including spinal fusions, non-union fractures, and periodontal regeneration.</p><p>The Demineralized Bone Matrix Market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 1.25% during the forecast period, driven by increasing incidences of musculoskeletal disorders, the rising geriatric population, and the growing demand for minimally invasive surgical techniques. Technological advancements in bone grafting materials and the expansion of healthcare infrastructure also contribute to market expansion. </p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift towards the adoption of DBM in combination with other graft materials to enhance performance and patient outcomes. Overall, the DBM market is positioned for steady growth as it continues to evolve with advancements in regenerative medicine.</p></p>

DBM putty offers a thicker consistency, allowing for better handling and shaping, making it suitable for various surgical applications. DBM putty with chips combines the benefits of putty with the structural support of bone chips, enhancing stability in bone regeneration and healing processes.</p></p>
