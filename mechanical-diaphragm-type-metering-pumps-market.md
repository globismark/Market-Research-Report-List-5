<p><p>Mechanical diaphragm type metering pumps are used to accurately dispense fluids in various applications. The market is segmented into two primary types: motor drive and electromagnetic drive. Motor drive mechanical diaphragm pumps utilize an electric motor to create diaphragm movement, offering flexibility and control for varying flow rates. In contrast, electromagnetic drive pumps rely on electromagnetic forces to actuate the diaphragm, providing precise and consistent dosing, often used in sensitive environments where exact measurements are crucial.</p></p>
