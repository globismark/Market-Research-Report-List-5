<p><p>Bleached Chemi-Thermous Mechanical Pulp (BCTMP) is a type of mechanical pulp produced by combining chemical and mechanical processes. This method involves treating wood chips with chemicals to enhance fiber quality before mechanically refining them, resulting in a higher brightness and lower screening content compared to traditional mechanical pulps. BCTMP is widely used in the manufacturing of high-quality paper products, including printing and writing papers, as well as specialty papers.</p><p>The BCTMP market is poised for significant growth, with an expected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 13.7% during the forecast period.
