<p><p>The textile enzymes market encompasses various types, each serving specific functions in the textile industry. Pectinase aids in the removal of pectin from fabrics, enhancing clarity and texture. Cellulase is used for cellulolytic processes, improving fabric softness and color. Amylase breaks down starch, facilitating better dye absorption. Laccase is involved in lignin degradation, enhancing fabric coloration and finishing. Catalase eliminates hydrogen peroxide residue from bleaching processes. Other enzymes may include protease and lipase, catering to diverse textile processing needs.</p></p>
