<p><p>Medium and heavy-duty truck steering systems can be categorized into three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering, Electric Power Steering, and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering. </p><p>Hydraulic Power Steering systems use fluid pressure to assist with steering, providing a traditional and reliable option for trucks. Electric Power Steering systems utilize an electric motor to assist with steering, offering better fuel efficiency and advanced control features. Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering systems combine the benefits of both hydraulic and electric systems, providing a balance between performance and efficiency for medium and heavy-duty trucks.</p></p>
