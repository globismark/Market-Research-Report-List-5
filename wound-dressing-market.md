<p><strong>The Wound Dressing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Wound Dressing</li><li>Advanced Wound Dressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wound dressing market is typically divided into two main types: traditional and advanced wound dressings. Traditional wound dressings include materials like gauze, tape, and adhesive bandages, primarily used for basic wound protection and absorption. In contrast, advanced wound dressings encompass innovative materials and technologies, such as hydrocolloids, hydrogels, and alginates, designed to promote healing, manage exudate, and reduce infection risks. The advanced segment often targets chronic and complex wounds, offering enhanced therapeutic benefits compared to traditional options.</p></p>
